Privacy Policy

Signal Cut Studio collects only the information needed to evaluate access requests, onboard approved creators or partners, and support short-form publishing workflows.

Information we collect

We may collect names, email addresses, channel URLs, TikTok handles, company names, onboarding notes, and descriptions of the applicant’s content rights model.

How we use information

We use submitted information to review access requests, verify whether an applicant is publishing owned or authorized media, communicate onboarding decisions, and support approved publishing workflows.

How we share information

We do not sell submitted information. We may share limited operational data with service providers used for hosting, email delivery, analytics, or platform integrations that support the workflow.

Retention

We keep application data only as long as needed for onboarding review, customer support, legal compliance, and service operations.

Your choices

Changes
