Workflow

Built around review, consent, and authorized media.

Source intake

Import owned videos or partner-approved footage, attach channel context, and log whether content is owned, licensed, or permissioned before editing starts.

Clip review

Generate short candidates, refine timing, and keep captions editable so creators can adjust text and hashtags before anything is sent out.

Approval and queue

Approved clips move into a queue with scheduled windows, status tracking, and manual or API-based delivery depending the connected platform setup.

Compliance

How the product handles creator consent.

Signal Cut Studio is designed for creators, owned channels, and partner-approved media workflows. Users must review or approve clip metadata before upload. The product is not intended for reposting arbitrary third-party content without authorization.

  • Creator identity is shown before export.
  • Caption and hashtags remain editable before upload.
  • Posting happens only after explicit user approval.
  • Publishing status is tracked after export.
